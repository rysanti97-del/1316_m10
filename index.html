Sea Turtle, Inc. 🐢

  • Hours: Plan 30–60 minutes for exhibits & resident turtles.
  • Cost: Budget-friendly; check current pricing.
  • Expect: Conservation exhibits, rehab stories, gift shop.
  • Why it’s great: Hands-on learning and cute turtles for kids & students.

Budget Eats & Snacks 🍤

  • Under $15: tacos, pizza, seafood baskets.
  • Family-friendly: combo plates, share sides, water pitchers.
  • Grab-and-go: deli subs, fruit cups, big waters for the beach.

What to Pack 🧴

  • Sunscreen & hats
  • Cash for fees
  • Water shoes
  • Refillable bottles
  • Light cover-up for evenings

Parking Hacks 🚗

  1. Arrive early for shaded spots
  2. Carry small bills/cards for meters
  3. Check park lots first on busy days
  4. Watch tide/traction on drive-on beaches
  5. Read signs to avoid tickets
